About Us

More Than Just a
Hospital — A Family

Amina's Hospital was founded in 2004 with a simple but powerful vision: to make quality healthcare accessible and affordable for everyone. What started as a small clinic has grown into one of the most trusted healthcare facilities in the region.

We believe that every patient deserves to be treated with dignity, respect, and kindness. Our team of over 50 specialists, nurses, and support staff work together every day to make that a reality.
